Eevee¶

Description¶

Eevee is a normal Pokémob.
Eevee evolves into Vaporeon:
- When given a Water Stone.

Eevee evolves into Jolteon:
- When given a Thunder Stone.

Eevee evolves into Flareon:
- When given a Fire Stone.

Eevee evolves into Espeon:
- When Happy.
- During the day.

Eevee evolves into Umbreon:
- When Happy.
- During Night.

Eevee evolves into Leafeon:
- When given a Leaf Stone.

Eevee evolves into Glaceon:
- When given a Packed Ice.

Eevee evolves into Sylveon:
- When given a Enigma Berry.
